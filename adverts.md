<div class="job-card">
  <h2>Internship Opportunity: Trustworthy ML & LLM Security (Attacks & Defenses)</h2>

  <h1>If you are sending email - make sure you have some expertise in this area, you already know about some attacks and defenses.</h1>

  <p><strong>Start Date:</strong> As soon as possible</p>
  <p><strong>Duration:</strong> 3–6 months (extendable based on performance)</p>

  <h4>Description:</h4>
  <p>
    This internship focuses on evaluating the <strong>security, privacy, and robustness</strong>
    of machine learning models, including modern <strong>Large Language Models (LLMs)</strong>.
    We are looking for highly motivated undergraduate or graduate students interested in
    studying and benchmarking <strong>attacks and defenses</strong> in ML systems.
  </p>

  <p>
    The project involves hands-on experimentation with real-world models, where you will
    reproduce, analyze, and extend existing attack and defense techniques. The goal is to
    develop a deeper understanding of how ML systems fail — and how to make them robust,
    reliable, and trustworthy.
  </p>

  <h4>Responsibilities:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Implement and benchmark <strong>attack strategies</strong> (e.g., poisoning, inference, jailbreaks)</li>
    <li>Study and evaluate <strong>defense mechanisms</strong> for ML and LLM systems</li>
    <li>Design experimental pipelines for reproducible evaluation</li>
    <li>Analyze robustness, privacy leakage, and system vulnerabilities</li>
    <li>Contribute to research reports and potential publications</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Requirements:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Strong programming skills in <strong>Python</strong></li>
    <li>Basic understanding of <strong>Machine Learning / Deep Learning</strong></li>
    <li>Familiarity with frameworks like <strong>PyTorch or TensorFlow</strong></li>
    <li>Interest in <strong>AI security, privacy, or trustworthy AI</strong></li>
    <li>Prior experience with LLMs, FL, or security is a plus (not mandatory)</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>What You Will Gain:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Hands-on experience with <strong>state-of-the-art ML and LLM security research</strong></li>
    <li>Exposure to real research workflows (experiments, evaluation, writing)</li>
    <li>Opportunity to contribute to <strong>publications or open-source tools</strong></li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Application:</h4>
  <p>
    Send your CV and a brief motivation to
    <a href="mailto:hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in">hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in</a>
    with the subject:
    <strong>“Intern – Trustworthy ML / LLM Security”</strong>
  </p>

</div>
