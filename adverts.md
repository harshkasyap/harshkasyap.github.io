<div class="job-card">
  <h2>Internship Opportunity: Generative AI & LLM-based Systems</h2>
  <p><strong>Start Date:</strong> As soon as possible</p>
  <p><strong>Duration:</strong> 3–6 months (extendable based on performance)</p>

  <h4>Description:</h4>
  <p>
    This internship focuses on building practical and deployable Generative AI systems
    using Large Language Models (LLMs). We are looking for highly motivated undergraduate
    or graduate students interested in working on LLM fine-tuning, prompt engineering,
    Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and automation of AI-driven applications.
  </p>

  <p>
    The project aims to move beyond demos and explore how LLMs can be reliably used for
    real-world, knowledge-intensive tasks. Students will gain hands-on experience with
    modern GenAI frameworks and develop end-to-end AI systems.
  </p>

  <h4>Responsibilities:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Design and evaluate prompt engineering strategies</li>
    <li>Fine-tune and adapt open-source LLMs for downstream tasks</li>
    <li>Build RAG pipelines using vector databases and document stores</li>
    <li>Develop automated AI workflows and prototypes</li>
    <li>Run experiments and document results</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Requirements:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Strong Python programming skills</li>
    <li>Basic understanding of machine learning and NLP</li>
    <li>Familiarity with LLM concepts such as prompting, embeddings, and fine-tuning</li>
    <li>Experience with HuggingFace, LangChain, or LlamaIndex is a plus</li>
    <li>Interest in building end-to-end AI systems</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Application:</h4>
  <p>
    Send your CV and a brief motivation to
    <a href="mailto:hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in">hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in</a>
    with the subject:
    <strong>“Intern – Generative AI / LLM Systems”</strong>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>Mentor:</strong><br>
    Dr. Harsh Kasyap (IIT (BHU), India) –
    <a href="mailto:hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in">hkasyap.cse@iitbhu.ac.in</a>
  </p>
</div>
